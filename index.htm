Help

This is an online snake game inspired by DeluxeSnake. Use the arrow keys to change the movement direction of the snake and try to collect all appearing food pieces. The snake grows by the value written on the food. Food with red numbers shrinks the snake.
Food is getting bad over time (transparent). For every piece you were not able to collect it's value is subtracted from your score. If there are too many pieces on the screen, there is a chance that the bad food will stay as poisonous confusion food. If you eat it, the arrow keys get inverted for 10 seconds. If you are lucky, eventually a bomb will appear. Eating it removes all confusion food items.
You can pause the game with P and go back to the menu with ESC.

Have fun! :)
