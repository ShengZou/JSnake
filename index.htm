Help

This is an online snake game inspired by DeluxeSnake. Use the arrow keys to change the movement direction of the snake and try to collect all spawning food pieces. The snake grows by the amount written on the food and twice the amount is added to your score. Red food shrinks the snake, but gives only the half of the points. Food is getting bad over time (transparent). For every piece you were not able to collect it's value is subtracted from your score. You can pause the game with P and go back to the menu with ESC. Have fun!
